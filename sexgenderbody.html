Sex, Gender, The body

For many years, the medical establishment has viewed babies born with atypical sex characteristics ashaving bodies that need to be “fixed.” As many as 1/2000 are faced with unnecessary medical interventionat an early age. Some intersex babies and older youth have undergone extensive, involuntary surgeries forno other reason than to make their bodies conform to traditional notions of what it means to be male orfemale.
